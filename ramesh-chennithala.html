Biography

Shri Ramesh Chennithala is a senior leader of the Indian National Congress and one of Kerala's most experienced politicians. Born on 6 August 1956 in Haripad, Alappuzha district, he began his political career through student movements and later became active in the Indian Youth Congress.

Over several decades in public service, he has represented the Haripad constituency in the Kerala Legislative Assembly and has served in various key leadership roles. His political journey has included responsibilities in governance, public administration, law and order, and social welfare initiatives.

As Minister for Home, Vigilance and Coir, he oversees important departments related to internal security, vigilance administration, prisons, fire and rescue services, and the development of Kerala's traditional coir industry. He is widely recognized for his administrative experience and commitment to public service.
