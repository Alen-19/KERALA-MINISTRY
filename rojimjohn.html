Biography

Shri Roji M. John is a prominent political leader from Kerala and a member of the Indian National Congress. He represents the Angamaly constituency in the Kerala Legislative Assembly and has earned recognition for his active involvement in public service and youth leadership.

Beginning his public life through student and youth movements, Roji M. John has consistently worked towards the welfare of local communities. His efforts have focused on educational development, infrastructure improvement, employment opportunities for young people, and strengthening public services across the state.

As the Minister for Higher Education, he is responsible for guiding Kerala's higher education sector towards excellence. His priorities include improving academic quality, encouraging innovation and research, promoting digital learning, and creating better opportunities for students to succeed in a rapidly changing world.

Through his leadership, he aims to ensure that Kerala continues to remain a leader in education while preparing students with the knowledge and skills needed for future challenges and global opportunities.
