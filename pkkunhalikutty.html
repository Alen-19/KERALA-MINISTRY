Biography

Shri P. K. Kunhalikutty is a senior political leader from Kerala and a prominent member of the Indian Union Muslim League (IUML). With decades of experience in public service and governance, he has played a significant role in Kerala's political and economic development.

Born in Malappuram district, he has represented the region in the Kerala Legislative Assembly multiple times and has held several important ministerial portfolios over the years. He is widely recognized for his contributions to industrial growth, commerce, infrastructure development, and employment generation.

As the Minister for Industries and Information Technology, Shri Kunhalikutty oversees key sectors including industries, commerce, information technology, artificial intelligence, startups, innovation, handlooms, textiles, and mining. His vision focuses on accelerating industrial investment, strengthening Kerala's digital economy, supporting entrepreneurship, and creating opportunities for sustainable economic growth.

Through strategic initiatives and industry-friendly policies, he aims to position Kerala as a leading destination for technology, innovation, and business development while ensuring inclusive progress across all regions of the state.
