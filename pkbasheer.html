Minister for Public Works

Shri P. K. Basheer

Party Indian Union Muslim League
Constituency Eranad
District Malappuram
Role Cabinet Minister
Portfolio Public Works Department

Biography

Shri P. K. Basheer is a senior political leader from Kerala and a member of the Indian Union Muslim League (IUML). He represents the Eranad constituency in the Kerala Legislative Assembly and has been actively involved in public service for many years.

Throughout his political career, he has worked closely with local communities in Malappuram district, focusing on infrastructure development, education, public welfare, and social development initiatives. His commitment to grassroots governance has earned him significant support among the people of his constituency.

As the Minister for Public Works, Shri P. K. Basheer oversees the planning, construction, and maintenance of government buildings, roads, bridges, and other public infrastructure projects across Kerala. His responsibilities include improving transportation networks and ensuring high-quality public infrastructure for the state's continued growth and development.

Through his leadership, he aims to strengthen Kerala's infrastructure, promote sustainable development, and improve connectivity and public services for citizens throughout the state.
